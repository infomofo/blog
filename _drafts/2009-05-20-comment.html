Once you find the feed you like, put it into your boxee profile (I have separate search feeds for each tv show I like. The metadata such as the image url is automatically added by boxee.


Select the episode you want to download. This list updates as the search feed adds info.


You can monitor all your downloads in a separate screen. This can happen in the background while you are watching other shows.


Once the show is downloaded, provided the episode is named in a conventional manner that boxee can recommend, it can automatically add metadata such as an episode title, number, description, and screengrab.
